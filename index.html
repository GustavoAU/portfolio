When should you consider an UX-audit?

  • The site has good traffic, but low conversion rate

    Metrics say that a significant number of people visit site, but only a small percentage take a target action

  • Poor user experience

    The site navigation is confusion for both you and your visitors

  • High cost per lead

    You invest in advertising, but user click on your ads without taking the targeted action

  • High bounce rate

    A high percentage of user leave the site after only a few seconds

  • No understanding of how to boost the site

    You are not sure what kind of improvements will benefits users
